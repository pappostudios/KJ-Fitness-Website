About KJ

Who is Kirsten Jemma?

My journey started in competitive sport, representing South Africa through judo and MMA, where I learned early on that strength isn't just something you build in training — it's something that carries into every part of your life. It gives you confidence, capability, and the freedom to move through life feeling strong and in control, especially as you get older.

Over the past 10+ years, I've worked across martial arts, group fitness, and CrossFit, coaching people at every stage — from complete beginners to experienced athletes.

Today, my focus is on helping people build strength that lasts. KJ Fitness is about helping you put yourself first and feel like the best version of yourself.

My approach combines functional strength training, movement quality, consistency and long-term progression. The goal is simple: help you move better, feel stronger, and keep improving over time.

Strength creates freedom, and that's what we're building.

What We're About

The KJ Fitness Approach

At KJ Fitness, it's not about quick fixes or short-term results. It's about building a body and mindset that keeps you strong, capable, and feeling good — for life.

Strength

Real, functional strength that serves you in everyday life — not just in the gym. We build a solid foundation that carries you forward for years to come.

Longevity

Training smart means training for the long game. We protect your body, prevent injury, and maintain mobility so you stay active and strong as you age.

Movement Quality

Technique always comes first. Moving well, efficiently, and safely is the foundation everything else is built on — power, performance, and confidence.

Consistency

There are no shortcuts. The real secret is showing up, again and again. We build routines and habits that make training a natural part of your life.
